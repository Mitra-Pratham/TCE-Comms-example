TeamConnect Enterprise v8.0

Discover what’s new in TeamConnect Enterprise 8.0 and follow a guided path for planning your upgrade, installing and deploying, and adopting new capabilities — all from one landing page.

  • Modern security and authentication with Oracle JDK 21, Spring Security 6, and updated SAML SSO.
  • ARIES™ AI digital assistant, improved logging and audit controls, and enhanced global search.
  • New Outlook Add-in 5.0 mobile support, Document Automation 2.0, Collaborati Spend Management 8.0, InvoiceIQ 4.0, and more.
